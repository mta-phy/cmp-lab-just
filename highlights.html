Research

Our research is structured around several key themes:

  • Spintronic Materials: Investigating materials for next-generation data storage and processing, focusing on magnetic properties and spin-dependent transport.
  • Energy Storage: Designing and simulating novel materials for batteries and supercapacitors with improved efficiency and longevity.
  • Optoelectronic Devices: Exploring materials with unique optical and electronic properties for applications in solar cells, LEDs, and photodetectors.
  • Sensing Materials: Studying nanomaterials for potential applications in gas sensing.
  • Wastewater Treatment: Exploring nanomaterials to remove toxic heavy metal ions in wastewater treatment.
  • Drug Delivery: Exploring nanomaterials to remove toxic heavy metal ions in wastewater treatment.
