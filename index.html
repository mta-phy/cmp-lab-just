Lab Overview

The Condensed Matter Physics (CMP) Lab at the Department of Physics, JUST is focused on computational research in condensed matter physics. We explore the structural, electronic, and thermodynamic properties of advanced materials using Density Functional Theory (DFT) and other quantum simulations. Our work contributes to material innovations in energy storage, spintronics, and optoelectronics.
